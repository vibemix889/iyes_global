Purpose is not only about what you do, it is about who you are becoming. Faith provides a foundation that keeps your growth aligned and sustainable.

## Listen first

Before you chase the next opportunity, take time to listen. Prayer, reflection, and counsel can reveal the path that fits your values and calling.

## Build with consistency

Small faithful steps compound over time. Consistency matters more than intensity when you are building a lasting purpose.
