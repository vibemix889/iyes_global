Youth entrepreneurship across Africa is rising fast. From technology to agriculture, young founders are finding creative ways to solve real problems.

## Common challenges

Access to funding, mentorship, and infrastructure can be difficult. Many founders also face limited exposure to markets beyond their local communities.

## Emerging opportunities

Digital tools, regional collaborations, and growing consumer demand are opening new doors. With the right support, youth-led businesses can scale and create lasting impact.
