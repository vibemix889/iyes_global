Ambition is not the enemy of faith, but it must be guided. When faith anchors your goals, ambition becomes a tool for service rather than self-promotion.

## Align your values

Evaluate your goals and ensure they align with the values you want to live by. This clarity helps you say yes to the right opportunities.

## Practice integrity daily

Small decisions shape big outcomes. Integrity at work is a daily commitment that builds long-term trust and influence.
